About the Project

Premium Freehold Living
at the JB–SG Gateway

Causewayz Square @ JBCC is a freehold mixed development by EXSIM Group, strategically located in Johor Bahru's CIQ zone — just minutes from the JB–SG checkpoint and the upcoming RTS Link connecting to Singapore's Woodlands North MRT.

The development comprises 4 towers: Tower A (Axis) focuses on short-term rental studios and dual-key units, while Towers B, C, D (Brixton, Coven, Dover) offer 1- to 3-bedroom residences with dual-key options for flexible living or investment.

Surrounded by City Square Mall, Komtar JBCC, R&F Mall, and Mid Valley Southkey — this is Johor Bahru's most anticipated cross-border address.
